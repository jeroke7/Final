About

This website was designed in Virutal Studio Code for a website development class. I programmed the HTML, JavaScript, CSS, and I created all art featured on this website.

Academics

I'm a high school student in Portland, Maine, and this was my first coding class I've ever taken in public school. This project was challenging, since, prior to this year, I had never coded in HTML or CSS, and I'd had little exposure to JavaScript in elementary school.
I'm a student whose interest lies mostly in the Art department, so this website was intended to showcase my artwork I have created throughout the years and give it a presentable platform for colleges to see in the future.

For college, I'm interested in exploring majors that are related to Art, the English language, or even other languages as well. I am currently an intermediate speaker of Spanish and am learning Swedish on my own with Duolingo, boasting a streak of over 800 days.
